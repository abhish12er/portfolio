I'm Prakruthi

A dedicated fashion design student at T. John College, graduating in 2025, I create pieces that blend elegance with individuality.

I specialize in conceptualizing bold silhouettes, sustainable styling, and aesthetic-rich visual fashion storytelling. My work speaks not just of fashion, but of cultural relevance and creativity.

I believe in details, drapes, and delivering styles that turn heads but also speak hearts.

Work Experience

  • Fashion Stylist Intern — Dazzeles Studio, 2025
  • Creative Designer — Young Trendy, 2023–2024

During these roles, I styled photoshoots, researched seasonal color palettes, coordinated model fittings, and collaborated with senior designers to curate unique, trend-forward looks.

I thrive in fast-paced environments, enjoy working cross-functionally, and aim to bring purpose and precision to every garment I craft.
